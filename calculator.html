    Did You Know? (Read this)

    👋 Personalized Greeting

    Double-click the Greeting text (e.g. Good Morning) text to enter your Name.

    ⌨️ Key Shortcuts

    Use keyboard shortcuts to navigate faster. Press / to search, V to voice search, Z for Zen Mode, T for To-Do, or 1-9 for your Shortcuts.

    🔗 Link Direction

    Open Link Direction in Settings to change the direction (New Tab / Current Tab) of Google Apps, AI Tools, Shortcuts, etc.

    🎙️ Voice Search Issue

    If you are using Gecko based browser (like Firefox), you can't use Voice Search. It's a privacy feature of Gecko based browsers.

    🔍 Switch Engine

    Click the current "Search Engine" (e.g. Google) to change Search Engine. Also click Open to open the Search Engine or the selected Platform.

    Keyboard Shortcuts

    Click a button, then press a key to rebind.

    Link Direction

    Choose where your links open.

    Location Access

    Your coordinates are used strictly to fetch local weather data. When you proceed, your browser will securely ping Open-Meteo (for weather) and BigDataCloud (for the city name).

    This data is saved locally on your device. We do not track you in the background, and we have no server to store your location data.
